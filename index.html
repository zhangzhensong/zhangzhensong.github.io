Biography

I joined Huawei Noah's Ark Lab after I obtained my Ph.D. degree from The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2018. Before that, I received a BEng. degree and a M.S. degree from Xidian University and University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2011 and 2014, respectively. I am broadly interested in 3D digital human related topics, including text-driven motion synthesis, audio-driven gesture synthesis, audio-driven face animation, human pose estimation, human nerf and et al.

We are looking for self-motivated interns, if you are insterested in doing cool 3D digital human project, welcome to join us, please drop me an email.
